Rescue

Project Title

In July 2014, this elephant name Raju was in the news because he was so moved by the fact that he was being freed from slavery that he cried. The organization that led the rescue, Wildlife SOS started a campaign called Raju's Rescue Fund in which people could fundraise money throughout the month of July 2015 for the rescue and care for abused elephants. I love elephants and am passionate about their freedom and care, so I joined the cause by selling prints of this painting of Raju. Visit my fundraising page if you'd like to donate!

Showstopper

Showstopper

One day, I was at a friend's grad party. She bought some cigars to celebrate her graduating with a nursing degree, passing her exam, and securing a job. Another friend was smoking one. He has this suave James Bond-esque air about him, and the fact that he was smoking a cigar just solidified the whole image. I felt super inspired to paint him, so I did.

Also he was drinking a diet coke with "Showstopper" written on it, which was perfect.
